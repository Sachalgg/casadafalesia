The Property

Five suites, one horizon

Built into a clifftop above the Atlantic. Raw stone, open air, and total silence — except for the sea below.

Exclusive use

Entirely yours.
No exceptions.

Casa da Falésia does not rent individual rooms. When you book, you take the whole house — every terrace, every corner of the clifftop, the pool, the kitchen, the fire pit. No other guests, no shared spaces, no lobby, no check-in desk.

It is a house first. It happens to sleep ten people extraordinarily well. The staff will cook, clean, and leave you alone whenever you want them to.
