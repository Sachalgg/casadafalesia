Getting here

Pipa, Rio Grande do Norte

Pipa sits on a sweep of Atlantic coast in northeast Brazil, famous for its dramatic red cliffs, pristine beaches, and the resident dolphin colony that patrols the bay.

  • Nearest airportNatal (NAT) — 90 min by road
  • From São Paulo3h direct flight to Natal
  • From Rio3h direct flight to Natal
  • TransferPrivate airport transfer included
  • Beach accessPrivate cliff path — 2 min from villa
