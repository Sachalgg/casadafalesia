Perched on the red cliffs of Pipa above a deserted stretch of Atlantic coast, Casa da Falésia is an architect-designed retreat — one of Brazil's most extraordinary and quietly guarded destinations.

Five ocean-facing suites, an infinity pool that dissolves into the sea, direct beach access. This is where you remember who you are.

Getting here

Pipa, Rio Grande do Norte

Pipa sits on a sweep of Atlantic coast in northeast Brazil, famous for its dramatic red cliffs, pristine beaches, and the resident dolphin colony that patrols the bay.
