Perched on the red cliffs above a deserted stretch of Atlantic coast, Casa da Falésia is an architect-designed retreat in Trancoso — one of Brazil's most extraordinary and quietly guarded beach destinations.

Five ocean-facing suites, an infinity pool that dissolves into the sea, direct beach access, and as many or as few activities as you need. This is where you remember who you are.

Designed to dissolve

Brazilian architect Rodrigo Alcântara drew from the land itself — raw laterite stone, reclaimed timber, and expansive open-air volumes that blur the line between inside and out. The result is a villa that recedes so the ocean can take centre stage.

Sport & Wellness

Move.
Breathe.
Be alive.

The coast here is one of Brazil's great natural playgrounds — steady trade winds for kiteboarding, long empty beaches for running, and Atlantic waters as clear as glass. There is a rhythm for every guest, from the ultra-active to the fully still.

Kiteboarding

The trade winds here are legendary. Equipment and instruction available directly from the villa.

Sunrise Yoga

Daily sessions on the cliff terrace as the Atlantic turns gold. Private instructor available.

Paddleboard & SUP

Explore sea caves, lagoons, and the coastline at your own pace. Boards always available.

Beach Running

Eight kilometres of undeveloped coastline in both directions. The finest morning run of your life.

Ocean Swimming

Crystal-clear Atlantic water with calm morning conditions and reefs that shelter the bay.

Spa & Recovery

In-villa massage, Reiki, sauna sessions, and holistic treatments. Recovery is part of the programme.

Getting here

Trancoso,
Bahia

Trancoso sits at the southern end of the Costa do Descobrimento — a stretch of coast where deep red cliffs meet dense Atlantic Forest and a sea so clear it shows its own floor. It is one of Brazil's most fiercely protected and quietly celebrated places.

  • Nearest airport Porto Seguro (PSB) — 50 min by road
  • From São Paulo 2h direct flight to Porto Seguro
  • From Rio de Janeiro 1h45 direct flight to Porto Seguro
  • Airport transfer Private transfer included with every stay
  • Beach access Private cliff path — two minutes from the villa
