Booking & Rates

Can I rent individual suites?
No — Casa da Falésia is only available as a whole-villa rental. All five suites, the pool, the terraces, and the full property are reserved exclusively for your group. This ensures complete privacy.
What is the minimum stay?
The minimum stay is 3 nights. During peak periods (New Year, Carnival, school holidays) a 5-night minimum may apply.
Is the price per suite or the full villa?
The quoted price is always for the full villa. Whether you are two people or ten, you rent the entire property.
What is the cancellation policy?
A 30% deposit secures your booking. The balance is due 60 days before arrival. Cancellations more than 60 days before arrival receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 60 days are non-refundable, though we always try to rebook the dates.
How do I confirm my booking?
Use our reservation page to submit an enquiry with your preferred dates. We respond within 24 hours with availability confirmation and a payment link for the deposit. The booking is confirmed once the deposit is received.

The Property

How many guests can the villa accommodate?
Up to 10 guests across the five suites. Children are welcome — please mention ages when enquiring so we can prepare appropriately.
What is included in the rate?
Daily housekeeping, linen and towels, use of all sporting equipment (SUP boards, snorkels, beach bikes), pool and sauna, Wi-Fi, and a private airport transfer on arrival and departure. A private chef can be arranged separately.
Is there a pool?
Yes — a 22-metre infinity pool overlooking the Atlantic, heated year-round. It is available to all guests at all times and opens directly from the main living terrace.
Is a private chef available?
Yes. We work with two exceptional local chefs who can be engaged for the full duration of your stay or for specific meals. Menus are built around seasonal Bahian produce and freshly caught seafood. Please enquire when booking.
Is the villa pet-friendly?
We welcome well-behaved dogs by prior arrangement. Please let us know when enquiring so we can prepare the property appropriately. Cats and other animals can also be discussed — just ask.

Activities

Can we arrange activities during our stay?
Yes. Our villa manager can organise kiteboarding lessons, private yoga sessions, guided dolphin-watching excursions, boat trips, massage and Reiki, and much more. Just let us know when you enquire.
Is kiteboarding suitable for beginners?
Absolutely. Pipa's trade winds are remarkably consistent and the bay conditions are well-suited to beginners. We work with certified IKO instructors who run personalised lessons from beginner to advanced. Equipment is provided for all levels.
Can we see dolphins from the villa?
Spinner dolphins are resident in the bay year-round and are often visible from the cliff terrace and beach in the early morning. We also arrange guided boat excursions to the Baía dos Golfinhos ecological reserve for a closer encounter.
Is there good surfing near the villa?
Yes. Praia do Madeiro (10 min walk) has consistent Atlantic swells and suits intermediate to advanced surfers. Board rental and local guides are available through our villa team.

Getting Here

How do I get to Pipa?
Fly to Natal (Aeroporto Internacional Governador Aluízio Alves, NAT). Direct flights operate from São Paulo (3h), Rio de Janeiro (3h), and other Brazilian cities. From Natal it is approximately 90 minutes by private car — we arrange this transfer for all guests.
Is the private transfer really included?
Yes, for all bookings of 3 nights or more. One arrival transfer from Natal airport to the villa and one departure transfer are included in the rate. Additional transfers (e.g. day trips) can be arranged at cost.
Do I need a car once at the villa?
Not necessarily. The villa has direct beach access and the main village of Pipa (restaurants, shops, nightlife) is a short walk or tuk-tuk ride away. If you plan to explore further along the coast or visit Natal, a rental car is recommended. We can arrange this.
Flying from Europe or North America?
The most common connections are via São Paulo (GRU) or Lisbon (LIS). TAP Air Portugal operates a direct Lisbon–Natal route (approx. 7h30). From the US, connecting through São Paulo or Miami is typical. Our team is happy to advise on the best routing for your origin.
